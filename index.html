About DPath.ai

DPath.ai is pioneering a decentralized platform that connects pathologists, patients, researchers, and AI model developers worldwide. By leveraging blockchain and AI technologies, we create a transparent and fair ecosystem where contributions are fairly rewarded, and access to pathology services and data is democratized.

Diagnostics Models

We accept payments via Stripe, Cryptocurrencies, and DPA Tokens.

The DPA Token

DPA Tokens are created when new data, annotations, or AI models are uploaded and evaluated&validated by experts. Each token represents a fraction of ownership of the products and services offered on the platform, ensuring fair rewards to all contributors and fostering a collaborative ecosystem.

Contribute & Earn

Upload data, make annotations, develop AI models, and offer teleconsultation services to earn DPA tokens representing your ownership and contribution.

Fair Rewards

Receive equitable rewards through token ownership, promoting a fair distribution among all contributors.

Trade and Buyback

Trade your tokens on the DPath.ai internal market and participate in our buyback program to redeem your tokens for fiat or cryptocurrencies.
