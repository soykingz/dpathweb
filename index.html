About DPath

DPath is a Singapore company pioneering a privacy-first platform that enables hospitals and pathology labs to contribute to global cancer diagnostics without sharing sensitive patient data. Our synthetic-first approach uses advanced diffusion models to generate high-quality synthetic biopsies that preserve diagnostic features while eliminating patient identifiers.

By 2030, we aim to empower over 1,000 hospitals and labs worldwide with AI diagnostics for 10+ cancer types, reducing diagnostic errors by ≥30% and cutting turnaround times from weeks to days. Our platform has already demonstrated 98% diagnostic concordance in external validation studies.

The DPath team consists of AI/ML experts from Carnegie Mellon University and University of Toronto, medical professionals from Johns Hopkins Hospital and Toronto General Hospital, as well as economists from University of Chicago and University of Cambridge.
