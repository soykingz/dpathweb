About DPath

DPath is a Singapore-based company revolutionizing cancer diagnostics through decentralized AI solutions. Our platform empowers pathologists with advanced AI tools, a streamlined pathology IT system, and remote consultation services to improve diagnostic accuracy and efficiency, ensuring better patient outcomes.

By 2030, we aim to partner with over 1,000 hospitals and labs worldwide, providing AI-driven diagnostics for multiple cancer types. Our technology has already demonstrated 98% diagnostic concordance in external validation studies.

The DPath team comprises AI/ML experts from Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Toronto, alongside medical professionals from Johns Hopkins Hospital and Toronto General Hospital.
